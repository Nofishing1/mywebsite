What's the difference between a "Normal VPN" and a "Gaming VPN"?
A "Normal VPN" is made primarily to bypass geo restrictions online such as Netflix Disney+ etc. Speeds are also not as strong as there are so many users around the world that use the same servers. A "Gaming VPN" is made for those who want a layer of security whilst playing online games. Every VPN Server is dedicated per client for Maximum stability and speeds. .
What does the word "concurrent" mean in your plans?
Concurrent connections are the number of devices that you can have logged in to our servers at any given time.
For example if you had a plan with 1 concurrent connection then you could have only 1 devices connected to our VPN servers at any one time.
What VPN Protocol does FilteredVPN use?
We use OpenVPN over UDP for the best Gaming performance, speed and DDoS protection.
What devices can i use the VPN on?
The Most Stable app is Pritunl which runs on Windows and Mac.
Does the VPN work on PlayStation and Xbox?
Yes but keep in mind that you will need a device to route the VPN through since neither PlayStation or Xbox have native VPN support.
You could use your Router/Modem if it has a built in OpenVPN Client or a computer running Windows, macOS or Linux, simply connect your console to the computer and share the VPN connection.
